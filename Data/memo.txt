"FFV_homopolymer.csv" and "FFV_polyamides.csv" contain the SMILES and FFV of 6589 homopolymers and 1457 polyamides, respectively. FFVs are obtained from MD simulations.
"All.txt" contains the smiles of all homopolymers, polyamides, and polymers from the reference "Revisiting group contribution theory for estimating fractional free volume of microporous polymer membranes"
